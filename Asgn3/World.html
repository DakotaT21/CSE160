Movement commands:
'w': Move camera forward.
's': Move camera backward.
'a': Move camera left.
'd': Move camera right.

Camera commands:
'q': Rotate camera left.
'e': Rotate camera right.
'click and drag' : Click and drag on the canvas.

Special:
First, place cursor in the middle of the canvas.
'c': Toggle pointer lock to lock/unlock camera rotation with the mouse.

Randomized map on reload.
